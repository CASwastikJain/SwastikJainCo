What We Do

Professional Services

01

Income Tax Compliance & Advisory

The firm provides assistance with income tax compliance including preparation and filing of income tax returns for individuals, firms, and companies. Advisory is provided in relation to applicable provisions of the Income Tax Act, 1961, including matters related to deductions, exemptions, and advance tax computations. The firm also undertakes representation support before tax authorities as may be required.

  • Income Tax Return preparation and filing
  • Advance Tax computation and payment assistance
  • TDS/TCS compliance
  • Tax law research and advisory
  • Representation support before authorities
02

GST Compliance & Advisory

The firm undertakes assignments related to Goods and Services Tax (GST) compliance, including registration, periodic return filing, and reconciliation. Advisory is provided on the applicable provisions of the CGST Act, 2017 and related rules. Assistance is provided in matters relating to input tax credit, place of supply determination, and representation before GST authorities.

  • GST registration and amendment
  • GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, and annual return filing
  • Input Tax Credit reconciliation
  • GST audit and advisory
  • Representation in GST proceedings
03

Audit & Assurance Services

The firm conducts audit engagements in accordance with the Standards on Auditing issued by ICAI. These include statutory audits, tax audits under Section 44AB of the Income Tax Act, and internal audits as may be required. The objective is to provide an independent assessment of financial statements and internal processes in accordance with applicable frameworks.

  • Statutory audit under applicable laws
  • Tax audit under Section 44AB
  • Internal audit and process review
  • GST audit
04

Accounting & Bookkeeping

The firm provides accounting and bookkeeping services to businesses and individuals. Financial statements are prepared in accordance with applicable accounting standards. Periodic review of accounts and preparation of management information reports are also undertaken as per client requirements.

  • Bookkeeping and ledger maintenance
  • Financial statement preparation
  • Bank reconciliation
  • Payroll accounting
05

Business Registration & Regulatory Support

The firm assists in the formation and registration of business entities including private limited companies, limited liability partnerships, partnership firms, and proprietary concerns. Assistance is also provided for obtaining various statutory registrations such as PAN, TAN, GST, and other applicable registrations.

  • Company and LLP incorporation
  • Partnership firm registration
  • PAN, TAN, and GST registration
  • MSME registration
  • Shops & Establishment registration
06

Financial & Compliance Advisory

The firm provides advisory on financial and regulatory compliance matters relevant to the applicable laws. This includes assistance in understanding regulatory requirements, structuring transactions from a compliance perspective, and preparing documentation as required by statute. All advisory is provided in accordance with applicable professional standards.

  • Regulatory compliance review
  • Documentation support
  • Transaction structuring (compliance perspective)
  • Liaison with regulatory authorities
07

Virtual CFO Services

Our Virtual CFO service provides growing businesses access to experienced financial leadership without the cost of a full-time CFO. We act as a strategic financial partner, overseeing your financial operations, planning, and compliance while aligning financial decisions with your business goals.

  • Financial planning, budgeting & forecasting
  • Cash flow management and treasury advisory
  • Investor reporting and board presentations
  • Cost optimisation and profitability analysis
  • Fund-raising support and business valuation assistance
  • Coordination with auditors, banks, and regulatory authorities
  • Implementation of financial controls and processes
08

Business Finance

The firm assists businesses in accessing finance through proper documentation, project reports, and financial structuring aligned with the requirements of banks, NBFCs, and government schemes. We help entrepreneurs and businesses prepare compelling financial cases to support their funding requirements.

  • Preparation of Detailed Project Reports (DPR)
  • CMA Data preparation for bank loans
  • Working capital and term loan documentation
  • MSME loan and subsidy (CGTMSE / PMEGP) assistance
  • Balance sheet strengthening advisory
  • Financial modelling and projections
  • Loan restructuring and NPA resolution advisory
09

Core Litigation – Indirect & Direct Taxes

The firm provides representation and advisory support in tax disputes and litigation before various authorities under both direct and indirect tax laws. With an in-depth understanding of the legal framework, we assist clients in navigating complex tax proceedings effectively.

  • Drafting replies to GST Show Cause Notices (SCN)
  • Representation before GST appellate authorities
  • Income Tax scrutiny assessment (Section 143(3)) support
  • Filing appeals before CIT(A) and ITAT
  • Penalty proceedings under Income Tax Act
  • High-value demand resolution advisory
  • Advisory on litigation strategy and risk mitigation
