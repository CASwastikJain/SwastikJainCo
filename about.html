Profile of CA Swastik Jain

SJ

CA Swastik Jain is a practicing Chartered Accountant and tax consultant based in 51A, Sainath Colony, Indore – 452001, MP. He is the proprietor of Swastik Jain & Co., engaged in providing professional services in the areas of direct and indirect taxation.

He is a member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) and has completed prescribed training and certificate-level courses, including Management & Communication Skills and Advanced Information Technology Training as mandated by ICAI.

His professional work primarily involves GST compliance, indirect tax advisory, tax law research, and regulatory compliance matters. He also undertakes assignments relating to tax planning and representation support, as may be required under applicable laws.

His professional approach is guided by adherence to applicable standards, regulatory requirements, and confidentiality in client engagements.
